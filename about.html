About EthioDrive

Revolutionizing vehicle rental and purchase in Ethiopia through technology

Our Story

EthioDrive was founded in 2025 by two passionate software engineering students from Wachemo University who recognized the challenges in Ethiopia's vehicle rental and purchase industry.

Traditional vehicle rental services in Ethiopia often involve complex paperwork, lack of transparency, and limited options. We saw an opportunity to leverage technology to create a seamless, transparent, and user-friendly platform for Ethiopians to rent and purchase vehicles.

What started as a university project has evolved into a comprehensive platform that connects vehicle owners with renters and buyers, making transportation more accessible and affordable for everyone.

Our Mission

To make vehicle rental and purchase in Ethiopia simple, transparent, and accessible through innovative technology solutions.

Our Vision

To become Ethiopia's leading digital platform for vehicle services, revolutionizing how people access transportation.
